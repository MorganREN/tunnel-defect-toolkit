## Provenance

This 20-image subset was selected by the maintainer from an annotated tunnel
defect image collection for documentation and software verification. The
selection covers the six foreground labels represented in the configuration,
single- and multiple-label images, and a range of source resolutions. Selection
details are recorded in `selection_manifest.csv`.
